Objection Handling

What they'll say and how to respond.

"I already have a website."
That's great — we're not replacing it. We're adding a smart assistant to it. One line of code and your website starts catching enquiries 24/7. Your site stays exactly as it is.
"I don't need AI / I don't trust AI."
Totally fair. Think of it less as AI and more as a smart answering service for your website. It knows your services, your prices, your hours. When someone asks a question at 10pm, it answers for you instead of them leaving and calling someone else.
"$49 a month is too much."
How much is one missed job worth to you? If the chatbot catches even one enquiry a month that you would've missed, it pays for itself many times over. Most tradies miss 3-5 calls a week after hours.
"I'll think about it."
No worries at all. I'll send you the full audit report so you have it when you're ready. Can I check in with you next week?
"Can I see it working first?"
Absolutely. Check out demo.layerops.tech — it's a working demo for a fictional plumber. Try asking it about blocked drains or booking a time. That's exactly what yours would look like.
"What if I want to cancel?"
No lock-in. Cancel any time. Month to month. We keep your business because the tool works, not because of a contract.
"Is my data safe?"
Everything runs on Cloudflare — the same infrastructure that protects 20% of the internet. Your customer data is encrypted and never shared. We can walk through the security details on a call if you'd like.
